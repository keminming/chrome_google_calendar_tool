Calendar++

User study page

What does it do?

Calendar++ aims to provide a faster way of adding new event to google calendar. It allows you to add new event by right click the content menu. It also nicely displays all the events in your google calendar and you can easily modify or delete event from the popup page.

Quick start

When you open a web page for example your gmail account. And you find some important time such as your interview day or UPS pick up day. You can just hight light the text containing the time information(y/m/d am/pm time zone), then right click the web page and choose the "add to calendar" on the context menu. The Calendar++ will automatically parse the time and if the format is recognized it will prompt you to enter the event title. Later it will create a new event through the google caldendar API. Then a success message will showed to confirm your operation. You can easily check the result by opening the popup page. On this page, all the events on your google calendar will be listed from most recent one to old ones. You can then modify/delete these events in place on the popup page instead of login to your google calendar account. The number showed on the icon indicate the number of event coming today.

Installation and permissions

Calendar++ requires two permissions: 'Access your data on apis.google.com' (to add/delete/update events and to communicate with calendar.google.com), and 'Access your tabs and browsing activity' (to access open tabs).

After installation, just click on the icon on the upper right corner of the browser and the extension will download event list from your google calendar and show it on the popup page.

Calendar++ communicates with google calendar It will prompt you to grant permission to this extension while you need to carry any operation to your google calendar account through this app. Calendar++ does not send anything else from your browsing activity to anyone, so you may rest assured that your privacy is not violated.
